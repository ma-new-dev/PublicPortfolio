# Privacy Policy — Simple Portfolio Tracker

**Last updated:** April 10, 2026

## Overview

Simple Portfolio Tracker ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

### Sign in with Apple
The App uses Sign in with Apple for authentication. When you sign in, Apple provides a unique user identifier. We store only this identifier locally on your device to maintain your session. We do not receive your name or email address unless you choose to share them.

### Stock & Portfolio Data
All portfolio data (stock holdings, watch list) is stored on your device and synced via your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This data is never sent to or stored on any third-party server.

### Market Data
The App fetches real-time stock prices and exchange rates from public financial APIs (Yahoo Finance, open.er-api.com). No personal information is sent as part of these requests.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs
- We do not sell or share any data with third parties
- We do not store any data on our own servers

## Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. You can delete all app data by deleting the app.
