Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a stock to my portfolio?

Tap the + button in the top-right corner of the Portfolio screen. Search for the stock by name or NSE/BSE ticker symbol, enter the number of shares and purchase price, then tap Add.

How do I add a stock to my Watch List?

Tap the Watch List tab at the bottom, then tap the + button. Search for a stock and tap to add it.

How do I delete my account and all data?

  1. Open the Portfolio tab.
  2. Tap the person icon (⊙) in the top-right corner of the screen.
  3. Tap "Delete Account & All Data" — it appears at the top of the list, highlighted in red.
  4. Confirm by tapping "Delete Everything" in the confirmation dialog.

This permanently removes all your portfolio holdings and Watch List items from this device and iCloud. It cannot be undone.

Does the app sync across my devices?

Yes. If you sign in with Apple, your portfolio and Watch List sync automatically via iCloud across all your devices. Make sure iCloud Drive is enabled in iOS Settings.

The market cap is not showing. Why?

Market cap data is fetched from Yahoo Finance. For some Indian stocks, data may occasionally be unavailable. Pull down to refresh, or check back later.

I can't see my data on a new device. What should I do?

Sign in with the same Apple ID used on your other device, open the app, and wait 1–2 minutes for iCloud to sync. You can verify sync status by tapping the person icon → Account Settings → iCloud Diagnostics.
